  • Locations: Kadana
  • Date: 2023 AUG 5

Soyuru Sathkara - Keleniya

In this project, we embarked on a journey to impart the invaluable knowledge of Mathematics to O/L students in the picturesque Kadana area. Over the course of two days, our dedicated team of approximately 20 members conducted intensive teaching sessions, not only striving to enrich the students' mathematical skills but also to inspire them towards a brighter future. The experience was not just about academics; we found immense joy in connecting with the students, sharing our passion for learning, and guiding them on their path to success. On the final day, we lightened the atmosphere with entertaining games, fostering a sense of camaraderie among all participants. As the sun set on our project, we concluded with a session filled with motivational speeches and invaluable guidance to empower these young minds as they set out on their journey to success.
