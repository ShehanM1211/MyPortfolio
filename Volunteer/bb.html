Beyond Boundaries-Anuradhapura

We, as members of Beyond Boundaries, successfully organized our very first chapter at Walagambahuwa Maha Vidyalaya - Anuradhapura on January 22, 2024! This initiative was all about giving back and inspiring the future generation. We had the privilege of conducting sessions where we donated school, sports, and sanitary accessories to support students' educational, extracurricular growth, and personal well-being. Additionally, we held a seminar for Grade 10 and 11 students to help them prepare for their O/L examinations and conducted motivational sessions on enhancing memory, exploring university opportunities in Sri Lanka, and adapting to the world of IT and emerging technologies. I personally had the opportunity to lead a session on "How to Adapt to the World of IT and New Technologies," where I shared insights on how technology is shaping our future and how students can leverage it for their growth. This was just the beginning, and we look forward to many more initiatives that empower students and create a lasting impact.
