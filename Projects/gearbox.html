Project Discription

The project aims to create a gearbox that can modify a motor's speed or enhance its output torque, specifically tailored for a sledgehammer application. it includes detailed sections on the problem description, background study, design calculations, and materials selection. Specific components like gears, pulleys, shafts, and bearings are methodically calculated and selected based on their performance in handling dynamic loads, wear resistance, and strength requirements. Additionally, the report includes extensive tables and figures illustrating the design, calculations, and final assembly of the gearbox, ensuring it is suitable for the heavy-duty operations of a sledgehammer in a blacksmithing context. This detailed approach aims to address the physical demands of using a sledgehammer, such as reducing the risk of muscle injuries and increasing precision and control in metal forging applications.
